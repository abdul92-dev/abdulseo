About Me

Practical, Data-Driven SEO That Actually Works

Hi, I'm Abdul Majeed, an SEO specialist focused on helping websites grow their organic presence through clear, practical strategies. I work with business owners, startups, bloggers, SaaS brands, local businesses, and agencies who want their websites to be found by the right people at the right time.

My approach is straightforward: I start with a thorough audit of your website, identify the most impactful opportunities, and implement fixes that make a measurable difference — whether that's improving your page speed, cleaning up your content, building quality links, or planning an SEO-focused content calendar.

I stay up to date with how search is evolving — including AI-powered search (AEO & GEO), Google's algorithm updates, and Core Web Vitals — so the strategies I apply today prepare your site for what's coming tomorrow.

How I Work

My 5-Step SEO Process

A clear, structured approach from first audit to ongoing growth — so you always know what's happening and why.

  1. 1

    Website Audit

    A deep dive into your site's technical health, content gaps, and ranking opportunities to build a clear picture of where you stand.

  2. 2

    Keyword & Competitor Research

    Identify the most valuable keywords your audience searches for and analyse what your competitors are doing well — and where they fall short.

  3. 3

    Technical + On-Page Fixes

    Resolve crawl errors, page speed issues, and on-page weaknesses — ensuring your site is fully optimised for both users and search engines.

  4. 4

    Content & Authority Strategy

    Plan and build content that earns rankings while acquiring quality backlinks to grow your site's domain authority over time.

  5. 5

    Tracking, Reporting & Growth

    Monitor keyword rankings, organic traffic, and conversions with regular reports — and continuously refine the strategy for sustained growth.
